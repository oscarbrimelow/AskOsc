Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Transaction Coordinator?
A Transaction Coordinator (TC) is a real estate professional who manages all the administrative tasks and paperwork involved in a transaction from the moment it goes under contract until it closes. We handle deadlines, documentation, and communication to ensure a smooth process.
Why do I need one?
By delegating the administrative work to a TC, a real estate agent saves valuable time—up to 16 hours per transaction. This allows you to focus on high-value activities like lead generation, showing properties, and negotiating deals, ultimately helping you grow your business.
What qualifications do you have?
My expertise comes from direct, hands-on experience coordinating 50+ residential real estate transactions and a deep understanding of the residential closing process. I operate with the highest level of professionalism and care, ensuring every transaction is handled with precision and attention to detail.
Do you work with commercial or land deals?
No. To provide the most focused and expert service possible, I specialize exclusively in residential real estate transactions. I do not handle commercial or land-only deals.
How quickly can you start?
I can typically begin working on a transaction within 24 hours of our initial consultation. For urgent situations, I can start immediately to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
What areas do you serve?
I primarily serve the Cleveland area and surrounding communities in Northeast Ohio. I can coordinate transactions throughout the region with expertise in local market conditions and requirements.
