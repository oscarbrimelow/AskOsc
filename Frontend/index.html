What to Expect

After we're all set up to work together, adding me to your team is easy.

Getting Started

All you need to do is add me as your Transaction Coordinator to a loop on Dotloop - or send me an email if you're working with another system. That's Oscar Brimelow and oscar@askosc.co.za.

Once I'm involved with a contract, I will:

  • "Clean" the file - providing complete titles to each document, so you can see what they are and the current status at a glance.
  • Send coordination emails linking Co-Broke, Title/Escrow and Lenders with all necessary documents and contact details. An email is also sent to your client to outline the next steps and to ensure they have all their documents.
  • Chase signatures and necessary documents, coordinate inspections (if requested), gather any information required as per the contract, coordinate and provide utilities transfer information, and ensure file completion.
  • Create and send your escrow letter to Title/Escrow.
  • Should you require additional addenda or want me to create the ROC, I can do this, with your instructions on what to include.
  • Upload all necessary documents and commission instruction to your brokerage's compliance system.
  • In the case of a non-closure, I will continue chasing the MR, and send it to the relevant parties before setting the file aside.

You will always be cc'ed on all correspondence unless otherwise requested.

What I Need to Begin Working

Here's what I'll need from you to get started

Brokerage Documents

I'll need your brokerage's forms. You can email them to me, or I can login to your Dotloop and grab them myself.

Login Details

  • Brokerage Compliance System (required) - to log your paperwork and commissions.
  • Home Warranty Program Login (optional) - if you actively promote home warranties.
  • Dotloop (optional) - if you want me to add myself to Loops (and, grab forms on your behalf).
  • MLS (optional) - Feel free to link to my MLS non-licensed personal account and I can make adjustments in the MLS.
  • Working with Docusign? I'll need your login details - and we'll need to coordinate my first login as a code will be sent to your email for a login from a new location.

As the industry changes and your brokerage along with it, additional logins may be required to get the job done.

Preferred Contacts

  • Have preferred Title/Escrow agents within a firm? Send their details and I'll automatically connect with them when the PA names their company.
  • Want help coordinating inspections? Provide your inspectors list and I'll work with your buyers and the listing side to get everything set up.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Transaction Coordinator?
A Transaction Coordinator (TC) is a real estate professional who manages all the administrative tasks and paperwork involved in a transaction from the moment it goes under contract until it closes. We handle deadlines, documentation, and communication to ensure a smooth process.
Why do I need one?
By delegating the administrative work to a TC, a real estate agent saves valuable time—up to 16 hours per transaction. This allows you to focus on high-value activities like lead generation, showing properties, and negotiating deals, ultimately helping you grow your business.
What qualifications do you have?
My expertise comes from direct, hands-on experience coordinating 50+ residential real estate transactions and a deep understanding of the residential closing process. I operate with the highest level of professionalism and care, ensuring every transaction is handled with precision and attention to detail.
Do you work with commercial or land deals?
No. To provide the most focused and expert service possible, I specialize exclusively in residential real estate transactions. I do not handle commercial or land-only deals.
How quickly can you start?
I can typically begin working on a transaction within 24 hours of our initial consultation. For urgent situations, I can start immediately to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
What areas do you serve?
I primarily serve the Cleveland area and surrounding communities in Northeast Ohio. I can coordinate transactions throughout the region with expertise in local market conditions and requirements.
